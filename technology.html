Our Services

Cutting-Edge
Geospatial Tech.

From discrete return LiDAR to cloud-native GIS analytics, we provide the full stack of spatial intelligence.

01

Aerial LiDAR Surveys

High-precision 3D point clouds and aerial imagery ideal for creating Digital Terrain Models (DTM), Digital Surface Models (DSM), and orthoimagery.

  • Technology: Riegl 780i LiDAR systems with high-density mapping.
  • Applications: Urban planning, forest monitoring, flood risk.
02

Drone & Photogrammetry

Accurate topographic mapping, volumetric measurements, and slope monitoring. We generate 3D models from aerial images for engineering projects.

  • Deliverables: Orthomosaic maps, 3D textured maps, volumetric reports.
  • Innovation: AI and machine learning for automated feature extraction.
03

GIS Data Processing

Transforming raw geospatial data into actionable insights. Includes raster-to-vector conversion, orthorectification, and geo-referencing.

  • Tools: ArcGIS, QGIS, ERDAS IMAGINE.
  • Processing: Cloud/haze removal, mosaicking, color balancing.
04

Advanced GIS Analytics

Deep analysis for complex engineering and environmental challenges. We turn maps into mathematical models for cut-fill, benching, and elevation.

  • Analyses: L-Section & X-Section, Cut-Fill & Benching Volume.
  • Modelling: TIN Modelling & Grid Elevation Models.
