Establishing the "Single Source of Truth"

Financial auditing in mining requires absolute precision. We employ a Hybrid Calibration Workflow where satellite data is rigorously benchmarked against low-altitude UAV LiDAR and Ground Control Points (GCPs).

Why This Matters

Stockpile volume errors translate directly to financial discrepancies. Our GCP-validated models ensure that the "Digital Twin" matches physical reality within millimeters, enabling auditable reporting.

Safety First:
Slope Stability Monitoring

Mining safety relies on predicting structural failures. Using high-density UAV LiDAR, we generate detailed Digital Terrain Models (DTM) to perform slope angle analysis.

  • Change Detection Detecting sub-centimeter ground movement between temporal scans to alert geotechnical engineers of potential landslides.
  • Grade Compliance Ensuring haul roads and bench slopes adhere to safety regulations (e.g., maximum grade angles).
