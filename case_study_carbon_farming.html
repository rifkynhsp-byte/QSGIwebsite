GEDI-Calibrated Biomass Model

Above-Ground Biomass (AGB) was estimated by first calibrating GEDI LiDAR relative height metrics (RH98) with field data to establish an allometric relationship between tree height and biomass.

This model was then extrapolated across the entire 92,000+ hectare landscape using Google Satellite Embeddings as predictive variables, creating a continuous AGB map with high statistical confidence.
